About

Hi! I'm Zekeriya Anıl Güven. I'm a computer engineer and assistant professor in Turkey.

I am interested in natural language processing, machine learning, language modeling, text mining, and combine of these research topics. I am focused on sentiment analysis, question answering, NLP tasks, etc. I have a goal of increasing studies on Turkish natural language processing.
